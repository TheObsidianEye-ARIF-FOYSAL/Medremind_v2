Features

Everything you need to stay on track

From your medicine cabinet to your adherence history, MedRemind keeps every dose organized.

💊

Medicine Cabinet

Track every medicine you take — brand, generic name, strength, and form — all in one place.

⏰

Dose Groups

Group medicines into Morning / Afternoon / Evening / Night schedules with exact alarm times and repeat days.

🔔

Full-screen Alarms

Alarms ring even when the screen is off or another app is open — with Taken, Snooze, or Skip actions.

📊

Daily Planner & History

See today's doses at a glance and review adherence over time with a colour-coded calendar heatmap.

🔐

Phone + Password Auth

Sign up once and verify your number via SMS OTP — simple, secure, and built for local carriers.

🇧🇩

BDApps Subscription

Subscription and OTP flows are handled through BDApps direct-carrier billing (Robi/Airtel).

How it works

Three steps to better adherence

1

Add your medicines

Search or add a medicine to your cabinet with brand, generic name, strength, and form.

2

Set your dose groups

Assign each medicine to Morning / Afternoon / Evening / Night with exact times and repeat days.

3

Let MedRemind take over

Get full-screen alarms at the right time, mark doses as Taken/Snoozed/Skipped, and track your history.
